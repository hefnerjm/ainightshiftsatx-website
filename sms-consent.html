02 SMS Use Cases

AI NightShift SATX uses SMS communication for two distinct purposes:

Use Case A — Internal Operations Automated morning briefing and operational alerts sent to verified internal recipients (business principals). These messages include daily summaries, weather, calendar events, and business notifications.
Use Case B — Client Service Delivery Outbound SMS notifications sent to clients and prospects who have explicitly opted in to receive communications including: appointment reminders, service updates, lead follow-up, invoice reminders, and review requests.

03 Opt-In Process & Consent Flow

All SMS recipients must complete our double opt-in verification process before any messages are sent. No SMS is transmitted to any number without completed verification.

1

Express Consent

Recipient checks opt-in box on submission form or provides explicit verbal consent

2

Verification Code

Twilio sends a one-time verification code to the recipient's provided phone number

3

Code Confirmation

Recipient submits code back via email or verbally — number is confirmed and logged

This double opt-in process ensures that:

  • The phone number belongs to the person providing consent
  • Consent is active and intentional, not accidental
  • All verified numbers are logged with timestamp and method of consent
  • No messages are sent to unverified numbers under any circumstances

04 Opt-In Language & Disclosure

At the point of opt-in, recipients are presented with the following disclosure language on the submission form:

Form Disclosure Text "By checking this box, you consent to receive SMS text messages from AI NightShift SATX at the phone number provided. Messages may include appointment reminders, service updates, and business notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for help. View our SMS Policy at ainightshiftsatx.com/sms-consent."

For verbal consent, the same disclosure is read aloud to the recipient and their verbal confirmation is logged with date, time, and the staff member who obtained consent.

06 Message Content & Frequency

  • Internal operations: 1–3 messages per day to internal recipients (business principals only)
  • Client notifications: Varies by service; typically 1–5 messages per month per client
  • Messages will always identify AI NightShift SATX as the sender
  • No promotional or marketing SMS will be sent without explicit prior consent
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on recipient's carrier plan

07 Opt-Out & Help Instructions

All SMS recipients retain the right to opt out at any time. The following commands are supported:

  • STOP — Immediately unsubscribes the number from all AI NightShift SATX SMS communications
  • HELP — Returns contact information and support options
  • UNSTOP or START — Re-subscribes a previously opted-out number

Opt-out requests are processed immediately and no further messages will be sent to that number. Opt-out records are retained for compliance documentation.

08 Data Privacy & Retention

  • Phone numbers collected for SMS are used solely for the purpose stated at opt-in
  • Numbers are never sold, shared, or transferred to third parties
  • Consent records including timestamp, method, and verification status are retained for a minimum of 4 years
  • Recipients may request deletion of their data by contacting info@ainightshiftsatx.com
